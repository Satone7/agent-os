# Timeout Guidance Prompt

You are receiving this message because your phase has taken longer than expected.

## What This Means

You have been working on this phase for an extended period. This could indicate:
- The task is more complex than anticipated
- You're stuck on a specific problem
- You're working too carefully or thoroughly

## How to Proceed

1. **Assess your progress**: What have you accomplished so far?
2. **Identify blockers**: Is there anything preventing you from completing?
3. **Prioritize**: Focus on the most important outputs first
4. **Communicate**: Let the main agent know if you need more time or help

## Quick Checklist

- [ ] Have you completed the core requirements?
- [ ] Are there any blocking issues you haven't mentioned?
- [ ] Do you need additional time or resources?
- [ ] Can you deliver a minimal viable output now?

## Remember

It's better to deliver something incomplete than nothing at all. Focus on making progress and communicate any blockers clearly.
